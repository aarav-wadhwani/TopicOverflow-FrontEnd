What is TopicOverflow?

TopicOverflow is a revolutionary software tool designed to create customized topic-wise question banks for college courses. Our mission is to make studying more efficient and targeted by providing students with high-quality, topic-specific questions that help them excel in their exams.

How It Started

TopicOverflow was born out of a need to provide students with a more structured and efficient way to prepare for their exams. Seeing the struggle many students faced in finding relevant practice questions, the founders decided to create a platform that would generate customized question banks, making study sessions more productive and focused.
